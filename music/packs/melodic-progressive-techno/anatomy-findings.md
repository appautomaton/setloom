# Anatomy Findings: Deprecated First Pass

The previous anatomy findings are cleared as melodic/progressive techno grammar.

The old pass compressed a small local corpus into numbers such as BPM, bass
occupancy, loudness, and break position. Those values describe a narrow local
sample; they do not describe the musical behavior that makes a track work.

## What Remains Useful

| Artifact | Use |
| --- | --- |
| `local/corpus/audio/` | Private listening and analysis source material |
| `local/corpus/stems53/` | Rebuildable layer lens cache |
| `local/corpus/dossiers/` | Technical measurements, not taste verdicts |

## Rebuild Requirement

The next anatomy pass must connect measurements to timestamped listening
observations. Scalar summaries alone are not accepted as style evidence.

```text
audio -> listening note -> layer evidence -> abstract musical move
```

Do not promote measurements into generator defaults until the human taste owner
has confirmed the musical behavior they are meant to represent.
